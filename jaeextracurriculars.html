Get Involved @ South Gwinnett!

Check below to learn more about the clubs and activites available at your school!

African Student Union

An organization that promotes African culture. We stress the importance of scholarship, service, and over all enlightenment of African culture to our members and to the community we inhabit.

Anime

Our mission/purpose is to learn about Anime, explore technology related to Anime, and interact with others who share these interests.

Art in Motion

Art in Motion will be rehearsing every Wednesday from 2:30-6:15. On August 18, 2012 A.I.M. will be featured in the Choreographer's Showcase featuring choreography by South's very own Jorge Castillo. Please see Mrs. Collins for ticket information.

B.E.S.T Club

B.E.S.T. stands for Bringing Extraordinary Students Together. The B.E.S.T. Club’s mission is to create an environment in which all students, those with and without disabilities, have more opportunities to build rewarding, positive friendships.

Beta Club

An organization who's purpose is "to promote the ideals of academic achievement, character, leadership and service among elementary and secondary school students."

Books Without Bindings

Books without Bindings creates an environment for connecting students and ideas by providing resources for all those who wish to pursue educational, community, and personal interests.

Caribsa

An organizations that enables Caribbean students to generate and foster a sense of unity and understanding among themselves, their fellow students, and the surrounding community.

Comets In Action

Comets In Action is one of the biggest volunteer organizations here at South Gwinnett High School. We commit hundreds of hours of our time to help the school, Snellville community and surrounding metro Atlanta area. We also run our Relay For Life team here at South Gwinnett.

Debate Team

Debate is about change. We are constantly engaged in a struggle to make our lives, our community, our country, our world and our future a better one.

DECA

DECA prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality and management in high schools and colleges around the globe.

FBLA

FBLA inspires and prepares students to become community-minded business leaders in a global society through relevant career preparation and leadership experiences.

FCCLA

An nonprofit national career and technical student organization for young men and women in Family and Consumer Sciences education in public and private school. FCCLA promotes personal growth and leadership development through Family and Consumer Sciences education.

Innovation Lab

An semi-autonomous organization that engages diverse participants—on a long-term basis—in open collaboration for the purpose of creating, elaborating, and prototyping radical solutions to pre-identified, systemic problems.

JROTC

JROTC provides a quality citizenship, character, and leadership development program, while fostering partnerships with communities and educational institutions.

Mock Trial

A program in which students participate in rehearsed trials to learn about the legal system in a competitive manner.

Relay For Life

Relay For Life a team fundraising event where team members take turns walking around a track or designated path to raise money and awareness for cancer.

Robotics Team

We are focused on building a better world for tomorrow by engaging high school students in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM). We seek to teach students the value of hard work, innovation and creativity. We go beyond competition by teaching teenagers the importance of working together, sharing ideas and treating each other with respect and dignity.

Science Olympiad

Science Olympiad is a national competition focusing on all areas of science. There are 23 events to choose from, covering everything from Life Science to Engineering and more! A team consists of 15 students (of which only 7 can be Seniors) competing in 3-4 events each. The events include building events, lab activities, and tests.

South Dance Alliance Dance Club

Come and join us at South Dance Alliance Dance Club where you can always feel free to express yourself through dance and show off your skills.

Spanish Club

Spanish Club usually meets on the first and third Tuesday of every month in Mrs. Howell's room (C302).

Student Council

Here at South Gwinnett, Student Council is a group of students elected by their peers to address issues of concern and organize student events and activities.
